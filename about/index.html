about

Nibir Bora is a graduate student at the University of Southern California, Los Angeles.

Also a Student Researcher at USC's Information Sciences Institute in Marina del Rey, where he is a part of the Computational Behavior Group, Bora focuses on understanding human behavior in a more intricate manner.

Bora's research interests include artificial intelligence, machine learning, sentiment analysis, multimodal human communications, spatio-temporal analysis and human behavior patterns. Although unconditionally involved with computer science, he often likes to delve into fields like psychology, anthropology and sociology.

Complementary to his work as a researcher Bora enjoys music, fine arts, photography and food.
