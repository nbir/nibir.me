Etola is a study in document clustering which addresses the unique properties of short text. We evaluate the effectiveness of traditional clustering algorithms on news headlines. News headlines, unlike bigger text documents, are constricted and succinct. They further do not guarantee correctness of grammar. Clustering news headlines may involve modified versions of the regular clustering methods as well as various heuristic techniques.
