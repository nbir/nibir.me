We are building a dataset comprising of 25 videos each of American, Chinese and Indian people in two person sit-down interviews. As interviews usually span a long time, we would trim the videos to instances containing agreement and disagreement. We will be limiting our study to interviews conducted in English language only. Although, interviews in native languages would be expressive (and more interesting to analyze), they would be difficult to annotate.

We will be looking at features from multiple modes of communication â€“ vocal, head movement, facial expressions and hand gestures. Annotation will be done fora window of 15 seconds which starts 5 seconds before the moment when the person being interviewed expresses agreement or disagreement.
